  • Chris Chul Moon

    General Manager

    Chris Chul Moon

    Chris Chul Moon is a vice president of diiPArt where he oversees whole projects and various matters in the office. With his 20 years of international experience in architectural design and construction fields, he has broad experience with a variety of program types including commercial, residential, hospitality, retail, mixed-use, institutional, infrastructure, and master planning. Chris’s expertise is in project management, and he has been the manager and team leader for many of significant projects including Seoul Financial Center in Korea.
    Prior to joining diiPArt, he had worked with numerous global-top architectural design and construction companies to conduct business in a socially, economically, and environmentally responsible manner of creating value for all stakeholders.
    He completed his studies at the University of Pennsylvania, graduating with a degree of Master of Architecture.
    He is a member of the American Institute of Architects (AIA) and National Council of Architectural Registration Boards (NCARB), and LEED Accredited Professional. He is a registered architect in New York, USA

  • Fred Youngmin Kwon

    Design Principal

    Fred Youngmin Kwon

    Architect registered in the Netherlands(SBA) and LEED AP. Born in Korea in 1976, Fred Youngmin Kwon studied architecture at Chung Ang University in Seoul and received his MSc degree in architecture from Delft University of Technology(TU Delft). After graduation he started working for Samoo A&E in Korea in 2005 and has practiced in the Netherlands, Seoul and New York as a lead architect until 2017. He joined diiPArt Seoul office in 2017, working on a number of important projects in Uzbekistan including Nurafshon Masterplan and new governmental facilities. As a project director, he has overseen a broad range of projects including Tashkent business center, New state museum of art in Tashkent and Samarkand hotel complex. He translated a book “Architectural positions, SUN Publishers, 2009” into Korean edition and has also lectured widely in universities in Korea. Mr. Kwon continuously works away at investigating more innovative design process integrated with other areas in construction and development industry.

  • Peter Yoonchul Cho

    Peter Yoonchul Cho

    Peter Yoonchul Cho is a registered landscape architect in U.S.A and Design Principal of DiiPart where he oversees Masterplan, Urban Design and Landscape projects in the office. He brings exceptional design and management leadership as well as an extensive construction expertise with his 20 years of international experience. Prior to joining DiiPart, Mr. Cho was senior associate at the New York office of Hargreaves Associates, leading Miami waterfront project, Duke University's Bryan Center Plaza, Las Vegas MGM Resort Hotel and Masterplan of American University in Beirut, etc. He completed his studies at the University of Pennsylvania, graduating with a degree of Master of Landscape Architecture and Regional Planning. He currently is a visiting professor of architectural design at the Hongik University. In 2009, He has received Project Honor Award from American Institute of Architects, New York Chapter. His recent works, Energy Park Masterplan and Leaf Trellis Folly Design, have been selected for 2017 Serbian Landscape Architectural Exhibition.
